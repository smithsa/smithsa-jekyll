200 W. Adams

200 W Adams is a 30-story skyscraper located in downtown Chicago. The site was built for Gerding Edlen, under contract with Shaker. The website offers information about amenities and layout of the building for potential renters. 200 W Adams was built in Wordpress and was built on a custom theme with custom field configurations.
